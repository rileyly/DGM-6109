This is one of the best scatterplots because it clearly exposes several trends at once: I take very few photos when at home. I mostly don't go out on rainy days unless I have to, for school or an errand. I'm more likely to visit other types of locations on non-rainy days, and typically take more photos there. As I visit more locations in a single day, I generally take more photos overall. Visiting 3 locations or at least 1 park appears to be the sweet spot.
